MEET ALFIE

At Alfie we believe education should stimulate your pursuit of happiness. Financing your education should not stand in the way of that. For that reason we introduced the Alfie Income Share Agreement.

Under Alfie’s ISA you pay a fixed percentage of your income over a fixed period of time, no matter what that income is. That way you will never pay more than you can.

HOW IT WORKS

Alfie provides students with tuition financing, in return for which students promise to pay Alfie a fixed percentage of their income over a fixed period of time after their graduation: the ISA is issued

The ISA is put in a fund, together with the ISAs of a large variety of other students. Based on the type of education, school, and job history, Alfie’s algorithms calculate the expected return for the entire portfolio

Alfie then sells shares in the fund to investors: in this way investors provide the financing for students in return for which they receive a low-risk type of asset that allows investors to diversify their portfolio
