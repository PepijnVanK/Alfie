MEET ALFIE

At Alfie we believe education should stimulate your pursuit of happiness. Financing your education should not stand in the way of that. For that reason we introduced the Alfie Income Share Agreement.

With Alfie’s ISA you pay a fixed percentage of your income over a fixed period of time, no matter what that income is. That way your paymnents will never be more than you can afford.

STUDENTS MEET INVESTORS

Alfie provides students with tuition financing, in return for which students promise to pay Alfie a fixed percentage of their income over a fixed period of time after their graduation: the ISA is issued.

Alfie uses proprietary algorithms to evaluate a student’s future earning potential, and then determine what reasonable percentage of income that student will pay. We also support students through a broad network of students, investors and employers to help students achieve their goals.

Investors buy into a pooled fund consisting of numerous ISAs, and in doing so provide financing for students. Because many ISAs are bundled into a common fund, investors can share in the successes of multiple students, providing them with a low-risk investment opportunity.
